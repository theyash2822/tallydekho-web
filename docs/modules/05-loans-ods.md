# Module 05 — Loans & ODs

## Overview
Track business loans and overdraft/cash credit accounts with EMI calendar.

## KPI Strip
Total Loan Outstanding, Total EMI/Month, OD Utilization %, EMI Due Soon

## Tabs
1. **Loans Register**: Lender, Type, Sanctioned, Outstanding, EMI, Next Due, Status
2. **OD Accounts**: Account name, limit, utilized, available — with utilization progress bars
3. **EMI Calendar**: Month-grid, EMI dots color-coded (Upcoming/Due Today/Overdue/Paid)

## Loan Detail Drawer
- Header: Loan name, lender, account no, type, status badge
- Amount section: Sanctioned, disbursed, rate, tenure, EMI amount
- Outstanding + next EMI date
- EMI Schedule table (scrollable)
- Collateral section
- Notes
