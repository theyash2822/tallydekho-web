# Module 06 — Reports

## Overview
Financial statements: P&L, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance with charts.

## Top Section
- KPI Strip: Total Revenue, Direct Expense, Indirect Expense, Net Profit, Total Assets, Total Liabilities, Closing Stock
- 5 Charts: Profit Trend (line), Expense Breakdown (donut), Income Breakdown (bar), Assets vs Liabilities (stacked bar), Sales vs Purchase (line/bar)

## Tabs
1. **Profit & Loss**
   - Summary cards: Opening Stock, Closing Stock, Sales, Purchase, Expenses, Profit
   - Expandable table: Income → Direct/Indirect, Expenses → Direct/Indirect/Admin
   - Totals: Gross Profit, Net Profit

2. **Balance Sheet**
   - Two-column: Assets (left) | Liabilities (right)
   - Expandable groups (Current Assets, Fixed Assets, Current Liabilities, Capital)
   - Side-by-side accountant format

3. **Trial Balance**
   - Summary cards
   - Table: Ledger, Opening Dr/Cr, Period Dr/Cr, Closing Dr/Cr
   - Expandable groups, totals row
   - Export: PDF / CSV / XLSX
